About Me
Hi, my name is Yang Yang (a.k.a 'YY'). I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia, where I have the chance of being advised by Professor Adwait Jog and being a member of the Insight Computer Architecture Lab.

Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree from Jilin University in Jun. 2023. And I was a member of ETECA Lab under the guidance of Professor Jingweijia Tan. Additionally, I was a visting student at State Key Laboratory of Processors at Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), where I worked under the guidance of Professor Guangli Li.

Currently, my primary research area revolves around GPU-centric system and architecture.

 

Interests
I am interested in the general area of Computer Architecture & Systems, including (but not limited to) GPU, Accelerator, Storage and Security.

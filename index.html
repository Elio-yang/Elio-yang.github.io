About Me
I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia, where I have the privilege of being advised by Professor Adwait Jog and being a member of the Insight Computer Architecture Lab.

Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree from Jilin University in 2023. And I was fortunate to be a member of ETECA Lab under the guidance of Professor Jingweijia Tan. Additionally, I was a visting student at State Key Laboratory of Processors at Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), where I had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Professor Guangli Li.

Currently, my primary research area revolves around multi-GPU system and emerging interconnect technology.

 

Interests
I am interested in the general area of Computer Architecture & Systems, including (but not limited to) GPU, FPGA for Accelerator Design, Reliability, and Compiler.
